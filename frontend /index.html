What is Pranora?

Pranora is a community-driven civic-tech platform that empowers citizens to report garbage hotspots, so volunteers can clean them up and make cities cleaner, together.

How It Works

1. Report

Spot a dump? Upload a photo and location.

2. Volunteer

Nearby volunteers are notified and take action.

3. Track

Get updates as your report is resolved.
